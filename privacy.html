Privacy Statement panda ads

At Panda Ads BV ("us", "we", or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Statement ("Privacy Statement") describes the types, sources, and purposes of the personal data we collect and use, including the lawful basis for such use. We may change or add to this Privacy Statement from time to time. If we make a material change to this Privacy Statement, we will update the effective date below and notify you by email or through our Service. This Privacy Statement is effective as of October 14th, 2024.

1. Who are we?

Panda Ads BV is a company that offers both tools and information that enables us to understand how Twitter and Facebook ads perform.

We may also use or display information that you share with us, for example when you authorize access to your social media channels in order to obtain insights on their performance. For more information about how we process such personal data, please see Section 2 below.

Panda Ads BV is the controller of personal data collected through our website (www.panda-ads.com) in addition to personal data collected through or from:

  • Social Cookies (Facebook Pixel)

2. What types and sources of personal data do we get from you?

Data Categories Data examples
Account Name, email address, phone number, IP address, device identification or profile information
Proprietary information IP, technical info, ad hashes, technical specifications
Usage Activity history, session and usage data
Financial Information Credit card number, billing address
Communication and support information Support tickets, inquiries, our responses

3. What do we do with your personal data?

We will use your personal data only for the purposes we collected it, unless we reasonably consider that we need to use it for another reason that is compatible with the original purpose. If we wish to use your personal data for an unrelated purpose, we will notify you and we will explain the legal basis which allows us to do so. Please note that we may process your personal data without your consent, in compliance with the above rules, where this is required or permitted by law.

We have set out below a description of the ways we plan to use your personal data, and which of the legal bases we rely on to do so. We have also identified what our legitimate interests are where appropriate.

Purpose Description and Legal basis
To provide you service and direct support We collect and use personal data in order to provide our Service, including to process registrations, provide customer support and improve your experience. We also use your contact details to communicate with you, send you notifications about service updates and remind you of upcoming deadlines or payment due dates. You can manage your communication preferences through your account settings.
Payment We collect payment information such as name on card and credit card numbers to process payment of any purchase made through our Service. The legal basis for this processing is performance of a contract.
Product Analytics We use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel and other analytics technologies to understand how you access and use our service. This enables us to provide and improve the service. For instance, we can identify underutilized features that we may consider removing or improving. Also, we may be notified about crashes or technical issues which enables us to improve the functionality of the service. The legal basis for this processing is our legitimate interest to provide you with good service.
Personalization We may personalize our service based on your usage patterns and preferences. The legal basis for such processing is your consent, which you can withdraw at any time by contacting us or through your account settings.

Your personal data will be stored on servers located in the European Union and the United States. Please note that data protection laws in the US may not be as protective as those in your location. When we transfer data to the US, we take reasonable steps to ensure your personal data is protected.

4. How long do we store your data?

We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. To determine the appropriate retention period for personal data, we consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the personal data, the potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure of your personal data, the purposes for which we process your personal data and whether we can achieve those purposes through other means, and the applicable legal requirements.

5. From/to whom do we transfer your personal data to other countries?

As mentioned above, we may transfer your data to servers located in the United States. When we transfer your personal data out of the EEA, we ensure a similar degree of protection by ensuring at least one of the following safeguards is implemented:

  • We will only transfer your personal data to countries that have been deemed to provide an adequate level of protection for personal data by the European Commission.
  • Where we use certain service providers, we may use specific contracts approved by the European Commission which give personal data the same protection it has in Europe (known as Standard Contractual Clauses).
  • Where we use providers based in the US, we may transfer data to them if they are part of the EU-US Privacy Shield which requires them to provide similar protection to personal data shared between Europe and the US.

6. How do we use third-party services?

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service ("Service Providers"), provide the Service on our behalf, perform Service-related services or assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your personal data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Third-party service providers we use include:

  • Google Analytics (web analytics service provided by Google Inc.)
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook Pixel - analytics and advertising tool)
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Various cloud hosting providers

7. From/to address, spam and other public authorities

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.

We may also disclose your personal information: (i) to comply with any court order, law, or legal process, including to respond to any government or regulatory request; (ii) to enforce or apply our Terms of Service and other agreements; or (iii) if we believe disclosure is necessary or appropriate to protect the rights, property, or safety of Panda Ads BV, our customers, or others.

8. What are your legal rights?

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data:

Right to: Description:
Right to access You have the right to request copies of your personal data. We may charge a small fee for providing copies if your request is unfounded or excessive. You can access most of your personal data by logging into your account.
Right to rectification You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
Right to erasure You have the right to request that we erase your personal data under certain conditions. Please note that there are exceptions to this right, particularly if we need to retain data for legal or accounting purposes.
Right to restriction of processing You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data under certain conditions. For example, if you contest the accuracy of the data or object to processing, you can ask us to restrict processing while we verify your claims.
Right to data portability You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions. This right applies to personal data you have provided to us with your consent or which we need to process for the performance of a contract.
Right to object You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data under certain conditions. For instance, you can object to processing based on our legitimate interests. We will stop processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds for the processing that override your interests, rights and freedoms.
Right of complaint You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe we have violated data protection laws. In the Netherlands, the supervisory authority is the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens. You can contact them through their website at www.autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl
Right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing You have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affects you. This right does not apply if the decision is necessary for entering into a contract or is authorized by law.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights or have any questions about this Privacy Statement, please contact us via email at privacy@panda-ads.com or by mail: Panda Ads BV, Prins Hendrikkade 21C, 1012 TL Amsterdam, The Netherlands. We will respond to your request within one month from the date of your request.

9. How long do we keep your data?

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Statement, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When we have no ongoing legitimate business need to process your personal data, we will either delete or anonymize it, or if this is not possible (for example, because your personal data has been stored in backup archives), then we will securely store your personal data and isolate it from any further processing until deletion is possible.

10. Changes to this Privacy Statement

We may update our Privacy Statement from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Statement on this page and updating the effective date at the top of this Privacy Statement. You are advised to review this Privacy Statement periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Statement are effective when they are posted on this page.
